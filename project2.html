The Garden Party

Adobe Illustrator

Our class was assigned a project involving adapting a short story into an artwork for the style of a movie poster. We were given too options: "The Nine Billion Names of God" or "The Garden Party". I chose the latter because there haven't been very many big-screen adaptions of the story, which presented me an opportunity to experiment with the tone of a hypothetical poster. This project was completed using Adobe's advance Image-Tracing Technology and designated Photoshop effects.

Poster for "The Garden Party", 2022
