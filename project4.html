Species Creation

Adobe Photoshop

Adobe Stock

Our assignment was to develop a new species utilizing stock photography. I opted to approach highlighting the slow nature of snails by replacing their shells with a contemporary alarm clock. All image assets were collection from Adobe Stock and edited in Photoshop to create the final result. Notably, this was the first project in this portfolio where I utilized gradients as a means of creating a background for this new snail.

Final Species, 2022
