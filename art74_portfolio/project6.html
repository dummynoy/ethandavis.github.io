Composition

Adobe Premiere Pro

We were tasked with developing an auditorial composition piece using royalty-free sounds from across the internet. The task involved creating a minute-long clip of sounds composed into a repeatable beat using Adobe Premiere Pro. Clips utilized were Jaws, Pink Panther, and Dr. StrangeLove (Or How I Stopped Worrying and Learned To Love The Bomb)

Composition, 2022
